Analysis pipeline

Every report runs this curated set of sequence-based predictors, databases, and structure sources.

Sequence & Prediction

🧬 UniProt Canonical sequence, features, cross-refs
🌀 PSIPRED Secondary structure prediction
💧 DMVFL-RSA Solvent accessibility (RSA / ASA)
🔗 NetSurfP-3 SS, RSA, disorder, backbone angles
📐 BLOSUM62 ΔΔG stability & SIFT-proxy scoring
🔍 HMMER Primate conservation & homology
🌳 Ensembl Orthologues, genomic mapping, exons

Structure Sources

🏗️ AlphaFold EBI pLDDT confidence & AlphaMissense
🔬 RCSB PDB Experimental X-ray, cryo-EM, NMR
🧊 3Dmol.js Interactive inline 3D viewer
🌊 Amphipathic analysis Eisenberg μH helix moment detection

Disease & Functional Databases

🫀 Human Protein Atlas Tissue expression, localisation, disease
🎯 Open Targets Disease associations, drugs, pathways
🧪 AlphaMissense Pathogenicity score for missense variants
🗺️ NCBI Datasets Transcript & chromosomal context
