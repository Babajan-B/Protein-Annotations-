Introduction

A focused portal for protein sequence and structural annotations

This site is designed as an academic bioinformatics interface for interpreting human protein variants. It brings together sequence-derived annotation, structural confidence, experimental structure references, residue-level context, and downstream biological evidence in a format that is easier to inspect than a generic dashboard.

The platform is intended to support a practical reading flow: identify the target protein, confirm the amino-acid substitution, place it in sequence and structure, and then evaluate the surrounding biological evidence. The annotations page is where that analysis begins.
